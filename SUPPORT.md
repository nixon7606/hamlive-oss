# Support

Ham.Live (open-source edition) is provided **as-is** for the community to self-host. There is
**no individual support channel and no commitment to respond.** Please set expectations accordingly —
see [Project status](README.md#project-status) in the README.

## Running your own instance

Self-hosting is do-it-yourself. Start here:

- **[INSTALL.md](INSTALL.md)** — install, local test drive, and hosting for your club.
- **[docs/](docs/)** — architecture, configuration, the operations runbook, and more.
- The app has a built-in **Guide** at `/views/guide` for end users.

## Questions & community help

- Use **[GitHub Discussions](../../discussions)** for questions, tips, and sharing how your club runs
  its instance. This is community-to-community help — answers aren't guaranteed.
- Please **don't** rely on direct or private support from the original author.

## Bugs

You're welcome to open an issue (see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)), but understand it may not be
triaged quickly, or at all. A clear, reproducible report — or better yet, a small pull request — has
the best chance of helping. Forks that carry fixes forward are encouraged.

## Security

For security-sensitive reports, please disclose privately rather than opening a public issue.
